The need is great:

  • 42% of pastors considered quitting over the past year*
  • 71% feel they are on call 24/7**
  • 63% say the role is overwhelming**
  • 38% say they feel isolated**
  • 31% say the demands keep them from their family**

Thrive in Ministry provides:

  • Ministry coaching for pastors
  • Counseling for pastors and families
  • Revitalization coaching for churches
  • R&R getaways for pastors and families

Pastors have the most important job to care for the souls of America. But who is caring for the pastors? At Thrive, we provide care, coaching, and counseling so pastors, their families, and churches are stronger, for the glory of God and the good of our country.

How the Newmans are equipped to help:

  • 26 years in church planting and pastoral ministry
  • 20 years of marriage and family counseling
  • Certified counselor with Association of Certified Biblical Counselors
  • Certified leadership coach with CORPUS Vitae
